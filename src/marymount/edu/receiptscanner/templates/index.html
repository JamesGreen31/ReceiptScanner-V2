How determinations are made

The parser crops sparse images to the content area, enlarges them, thresholds the result, then OCRs the processed image. It finds every recognized currency amount and uses the highest dollar figure as the total. Dates are normalized to YYYY-MM-DD when they match common numeric or month-name formats.

A receipt is marked needs_review and highlighted yellow when a required field is missing. Those records stay out of the monthly summary until corrected. Reset All clears the JSON store completely.
